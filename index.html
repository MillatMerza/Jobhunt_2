Experience 3D in 4 Easy Steps

Follow these steps to access and interact with products in a fully immersive 3D experience.

Scan a QR Code

Use your smartphone or device to scan the unique QR code for any product you want to explore.

View the 3D Model

Get instant access to the 3D model, rotating, zooming, and inspecting the product in real-time.

Explore in AR

Interact and Customize
